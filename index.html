Core Features

Built for When It Matters Most

Every feature designed for zero-infrastructure emergency scenarios

One-Tap SOS

Single tap broadcasts your emergency across the entire mesh network instantly — no internet required.

Mesh Networking

Bluetooth Low Energy + WiFi Direct create a self-healing mesh. Each device is a relay node.

AI Prioritization

On-device AI scores and ranks emergencies by severity, proximity, and response capacity in real time.

Volunteer Mode

Community volunteers get notified of nearby emergencies and can accept response requests with one tap.

Location Sharing

GPS coordinates propagate through the mesh with each SOS hop, letting rescuers navigate accurately.

Fully Offline

Zero dependency on cellular, internet, or any centralized infrastructure. Works during total blackouts.

How ResQNet Works

From your finger to a volunteer in seconds — no towers, no internet

01

User Presses SOS

Victim taps the SOS button. Alert is generated with GPS coordinates, timestamp, and device ID.

→
02

Broadcast via BLE/WiFi

Signal broadcasts over Bluetooth Mesh and WiFi Direct to all devices within range simultaneously.

→
03

Devices Forward Signal

Each receiving device re-broadcasts the alert, extending range hop-by-hop across the mesh network.

→
04

Volunteer Responds

Nearest available volunteer receives alert with AI priority score and navigates to the victim's location.

About This Project

ResQNet was designed to solve a critical gap in emergency response: when natural disasters, power outages, or infrastructure failures knock out cellular networks, people still need to call for help. By leveraging existing smartphone hardware (BLE + WiFi) to form a spontaneous mesh network, ResQNet ensures emergency communications never go dark.
